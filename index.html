About

Hi, I'm AJ.

I help honest local businesses get online, the simple way.

These days a website isn't really optional. Usually you've got two ways to get one, and both have real downsides. Build it yourself with a website-builder app and you're stuck inside their platform, usually paying every month to keep it running. Hire an agency and you'll often get pushed toward extra services and marketing you never asked for, which gets overwhelming fast.

I built gobeefound to be the simpler, honest option. I make you a professional website that's easy for customers to find and get in touch with, and most of all, it's YOURS. Simple and honest, that's the whole idea, and those are exactly the businesses I want to help.
